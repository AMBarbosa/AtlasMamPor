Extracted to UTM 10x10 km2 cells or Portuguese islands (using the 'terra' R package) from the mammal presences in GBIF (downloaded 2023/09/14) where institutionCode was ICNF, collectionCode was red_book_of_mammals* (Livro Vermelho dos Mamíferos de Portugal Continental, https://repositorio.ul.pt/handle/10451/58108), and coordinateUncertaintyInMeters was up to the distance from the centre to the corner of a 10x10 km2 square.
